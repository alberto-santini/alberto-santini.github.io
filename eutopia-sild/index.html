“Sustainable Integrated Last-Mile Delivery”

There's a package for you!

E-commerce has disrupted the last segment of our supply chains. Instead of shipping a few bulky pallets to stores, couriers now delivery many small and light packets directly at our doorstep.

Same-day delivery is here to stay

The COVID-19 pandemic has proven the importance of resilient last-mile supply chains. Businesses must guarantee fast, on-time delivery of all sorts of products, including prepared food, groceries and consumer goods. We develop optimisation tools for the highly dynamic world of modern last-mile delivery.

We're all in this together

While many people enjoy the benefits of e-commerce, we all suffer from its externalities: traffic congestion, noise, pollution. Let's show that a more sustainable approach is possible for last-mile delivery in dense urban environments!

The SILD Project

 

Who we are

I am Alberto Santini, a EUTOPIA-SIF fellow at CY Cergy Université. Together with my host Claudia Archetti and co-host Tolga Bektas, we work on making freight transportation more sustainable, so that everyone can enjoy its convenience.

 

What we do

We use advanced optimisation techniques, combined with machine learning and insights from economics, to build decision support tools for couriers and e-commerces.

 

What we achieve

We disseminate our work through scientific publications, workshops and meetings with regulators and businesses. We also communicate our results to the general public.
