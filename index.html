Alberto Santini

About me

I am a tenure-track assistant professor at Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona, Spain. I am also an affiliated professor at the Barcelona Graduate School of Mathematics and Barcelona Graduate School of Economics.

I founded AIROYoung and EUROYoung, two chapters bringing together young Operational Research scientists in Italy and Europe, respectively. I also sit on the board of directors of AIRO, the Italian Operational Research Association.
