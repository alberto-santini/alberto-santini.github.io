# The Department

You will be joining the department of Economics and Business at Universitat Pompeu Fabra.
Some interesting facts about our department:

* We are ranked 37th worldwide and 12th in Europe among Econ departments (Tilburg University EconTop ranking). We are ranked first in Spain by virtually all rankings.
* We have around 100 professors (full, associate, and tenure-track assistants).
* As of December 2025, we have 15 active ERC grants (5 starting, 5 consolidator, and 5 advanced).

Many department members are also cross-appointed at other UPF-affiliated institutions as:

* Affiliate professors and/or part-time lecturers at the [Barcelona School of Economics](https://bse.eu/).
* Affiliate professors at the [Barcelona Graduate School of Mathematics](https://bgsmath.cat/).
* Part-time lecturers at the [Barcelona School of Management](https://www.bsm.upf.edu/es).

Most people working in Operations Research in our department are also part of the [Data Science Centre](https://datascience.bse.eu/) at the Barcelona School of Economics.
